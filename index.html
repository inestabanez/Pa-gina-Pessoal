who am i?

Hi there! My name is Inês Tabanez and I’m a 22 year-old girl from Portugal. Currently, I am finishing my Master’s Degree in Design and Multimedia at the University of Coimbra. I also have a degree in Computer Science. I have a huge passion for web development since I get to mix my two main interests: design and programming. When I am not sitting in front of my laptop, I love spending quality time with my family, my friends and my pets, exploring new places and bringing a camera with me to capture it all. Aditionally, I am passionate about cinema, environmental sustainability and I'm way more interested in pop culture than I should (oops).

what am i about?

I think it's clear by now how much I am obsessed with colors and shapes, especially circular and trippy ones. Exploring collages with multiple layer arrangements is also something that I tend to enjoy in my design creations. The design of this personal web page is inspired by all of these things. I created four shapes to represent four of my traits, which are mentioned in this section. I used them as a base to make a web experience for all of you, so that you can get an idea of what my design process is like. I encourage you to interact with the shapes on the welcome screen and make your own unique designs. You can drag the shapes all the way down to the end of the website! Go crazy with it and mostly, have fun!

Perfeccionism

I am extremely critical of myself, which is both a good and a not so great thing. Although I put so much pressure and stress on my shoulders to make things exactly as I want them to, when I take my time, I usually end up with something that is the best I can do it and something that I'm proud of. That feeling is irreplaceable.

Emotion

Everything that I do has a lot of emotion behind it. I am always told how emotional I am but I take that as compliment because it makes me express myself that much better and it means that I care. I am able to take all of that emotion and learn so much from it, making me evolve constantly in every aspect of my life. I love when I get to explore emotions in my projects, to make people feel something and connect with them.

Genorosity

Helping people is one of things that brings me the biggest joy in life. If I have the capability to make someone's day a little better, then I will 100% take that chance. I deeply believe that this world needs more tolerance and kindness between people. After all, we all have so much to offer and so much to receive from the ones around us and that makes our lives that much meaningful.

Deepness

Like my designs, I have a lot of layers. I spend a lot of time overthinking and trying to get answers to all of my questions. I am an extremely curious person, which I consider one of my best traits. Although I sometimes get stuck on my thoughts, reflecting so deeply and having interest in all kinds of things makes me so much more complex and wise and I get to make better decisions more carefully.
